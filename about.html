About Us

SRI MIRRA FURNITURE is a trusted name in furniture industry for over 25 years. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design, using premium materials to create pieces that are both elegant and durable.

Our collection ranges from ergonomic chairs to functional office furniture, with options for custom-made and ready-to-assemble designs. Every piece is crafted with care to meet diverse needs and preferences. At SRI MIRRA FURNITURE, customer satisfaction is our priority.

We offer a personalized experience to help you find furniture that truly complements your workspace. Join the thousands who rely on us for quality, trust, and long-lasting design and comfort.
